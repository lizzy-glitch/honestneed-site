What is Honestneed?

Honestneed is a grassroots, U.S.-based nonprofit that provides small, emergency grants to hardworking families at risk of slipping into poverty — those who are often overlooked by traditional aid programs.

Since our founding in 2024, we've helped prevent homelessness, utilities shutoffs, and food insecurity for families just one step away from crisis.
