About Honestneed

Founded in 2010, Honestneed is a non-profit organization with a heartfelt mission to uplift hardworking individuals and families facing unexpected short-term crises. We believe that no one should fall into poverty because of a single emergency — and with your help, we stop that from happening.

Honestneed exists:

  • To provide short-term financial assistance to working individuals and families who live just above the poverty line but are often ineligible for traditional help.
  • To help prevent these at-risk households from falling into deeper financial trouble or homelessness by stepping in during urgent situations.
  • To inspire compassion and generosity by connecting donors directly with the real people they’re helping across the United States.

We proudly support school teachers, veterans, first responders, single parents, and everyday workers who contribute to their communities but find themselves in temporary hardship.

Importantly: We never send money directly to applicants. When our donors support someone, we make payment directly to the landlord, utility company, or vendor listed in the application. That way, your help always goes where it's truly needed.

Honestneed stands as living proof that human kindness — no matter how big or small — can change lives.
