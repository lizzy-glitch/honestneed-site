About Honestneed

Founded in 2025, Honestneed was created to serve Americans who work hard but earn just above the poverty line — those overlooked by government aid during emergencies.

We exist with three core goals:

  • To offer small, short-term grants to individuals without access to traditional assistance
  • To prevent at-risk families from slipping into homelessness or crisis following unexpected events
  • To promote generosity by connecting everyday donors with real people in need

We operate with transparency and responsibility—grant funds are sent directly to vendors or service providers, not given as cash.
