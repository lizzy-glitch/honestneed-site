About Honestneed

Founded in 2010, Honestneed is a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping hardworking families and individuals in the United States navigate short-term financial crises without falling deeper into poverty. We bridge the gap between urgent need and generous hearts by connecting vetted applicants with donors who want to make a direct impact.

From rent assistance and utility bills to emergency repairs and food support, Honestneed believes that no one should suffer due to a temporary setback. Every story is real. Every donation counts.
